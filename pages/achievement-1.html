Context

The Enbridge Engineering Scholarship is an annual award within York Universityâ€™s Lassonde School of Engineering that supports entering or continuing engineering students. It is a university-level scholarship that recognizes students who have demonstrated academic promise while also meeting financial need and residency requirements.

This scholarship mattered to me because it validated the effort I had invested before university and reinforced that I was entering engineering with momentum. Receiving this scholarship confirmed that consistent effort and strong fundamentals create opportunities long before the most visible achievements begin.
