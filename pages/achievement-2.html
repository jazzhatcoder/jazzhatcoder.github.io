Ontario Professional Engineers Foundation for Education Undergraduate Scholarship

A $1,500 scholarship awarded annually to Lassonde engineering students in second through final year who demonstrate strong academic standing alongside leadership, engagement, or service.

Context

The Ontario Professional Engineers Foundation for Education Undergraduate Scholarship is an annual academic scholarship offered to engineering students in the Lassonde School of Engineering at York University. It is awarded to two students in second, third, fourth, or final year who maintain a minimum GPA of 7.5 and demonstrate qualities such as financial need, engineering aptitude, leadership in professional affairs, sustained extracurricular involvement, or continued volunteerism.

Significance

In the context of a demanding engineering program, this scholarship represents more than strong grades alone. It reflects consistent effort across coursework, technical growth, and broader involvement beyond the classroom, recognizing the kind of well-rounded development that is especially important in engineering where academic performance, initiative, and professional engagement all matter.

The Work Behind It

Earning this recognition was the result of disciplined study habits, sustained technical curiosity, and a willingness to stay engaged in engineering work outside formal coursework. Balancing academic demands with research, hands-on projects, and extracurricular involvement strengthened my ability to manage open-ended problems, learn quickly, and maintain a high standard across both technical and professional commitments.

This scholarship signals that I bring not only strong academic performance, but also the initiative, consistency, and broader engagement needed to grow into a well-rounded engineer.
